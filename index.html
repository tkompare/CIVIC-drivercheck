More Information

Driver Check - Chicago is a convenient way to look up if your car service driver has an active public chauffeur license issued by the City of Chicago. Some types of car services require an active license. Drive Check - Chicago does not check to see if a driver has a valid state issued driver's license.

There are two types of public chauffeur licenses the Driver Check - Chicago looks for, depending on the car service. For example, a driver offering taxi services requires an unrestricted public chauffeur license (a "taxi" license). A driver offering UberBlack requires a public chauffeur license that is either unrestricted or restricted (a "livery only" license).

If your chosen car service does not require a public chauffeur license, you may still be interested in checking to see if your driver has a public chauffeur license and its status.

Driver Check - Chicago uses the City of Chicago Data Portal as its system of record to look up license status. For more information about public chauffeur licenses see the links below:
